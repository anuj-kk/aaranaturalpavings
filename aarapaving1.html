About Us

Different types of stones and pebbles are used for the purpose of construction of buildings. These are also used to furnish place like homes and offices. In both the cases, the stones and pebbles need to be of optimum quality. Customers always look for the most reliable supplier of these items to satisfy their needs. Quality Assurance


Our offerings are trusted because of their quality. This is due to our concern to maintain our reputation as a supplier whose range is matchless in terms of quality. The Natural Sandstone Slab and pebbles manufactured in our company are tested on various parameters. These tests are conducted by our quality control team, which makes sure that the stones are high in quality. Following are tests by which are range goes through:

Absorption and resistance test
Modulus of Rupture test
Compressive Strength Test
Flexural Ability Test
Modulus of Elasticity
Individual pebbles Anchorages Test
